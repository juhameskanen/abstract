What is the most compressible transition from initial zero entropy wavefunction to this maximal entropy state one?
It is the least action path, not through physical coordinates, but through the configuration space.

the observer will inhabit (we measure?) the version of the history that minimizes the integrated complexity

  This looks like Symmetry Breaking and the emergence of nested structures.
\item \textbf{Locality:} To keep the geometric cost low, these modes must interfere in a way that creates "peaks"—which we perceive as Particles and Objects.
\end{itemize}

\section*{The Variational Principle of Information}

The core assertion of this idea is that the history of the universe is the solution to a variational
problem where the objective function is informational complexity rather than physical action. 

The observed universe is defined as the path through configuration space that minimizes the integrated spectral complexity:

\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{U}_{\text{obs}} = \arg\min_{\text{paths}} \int_{t_0}^{t_{f}} \mathcal{C}_{\text{spectral}}(\Psi_t) \, dt
\end{equation}

\noindent subject to the following constraints:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Boundary Conditions:} A transition from a zero-entropy initial state $\Psi_{t_0}$ (all zeros) to a high-entropy final state $\Psi_{t_{f}}$ (thermal noise).
    \item \textbf{Observer Weighting:} The probability measure of any specific history $\gamma$ is weighted by the Solomonoff prior:
    \begin{equation}
        \mathbb{P}(\gamma \mid O) \propto 2^{-\mathcal{C}_O(\gamma)}
    \end{equation}
\end{itemize}

This formulation provides a unified basis for three pillars of modern physics:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{The Principle of Least Action:} Classical trajectories emerge as the "shortest descriptions" of moving matter.
    \item \textbf{Path Integral Dominance:} In the quantum regime, the most probable "paths" are those where the spectral interference minimizes the description length.
    \item \textbf{The Arrow of Time:} The transition from low to high entropy is not an accidental evolution, but the most compressible interpolation between "Nothing" and "Everything."
\end{enumerate}

In this view, the "Action" is simply the rate at which an observer must update their internal model to track the state. Physics is the study of how the universe conserves its own description length.
